## 🌙 About

I’m Brittany, a graphic designer focused on **web design and UI/UX**.

I care about:
- clarity over clutter  
- typography that actually matters  
- layouts that feel balanced without trying too hard  

Most of my work lives between **clean structure** and **soft personality**.

---

## ✦ Design Approach

```
less noise
more intention
```

- grid-based layouts  
- soft contrast instead of harsh colors  
- small details that feel intentional  
- designs that feel calm, not chaotic
